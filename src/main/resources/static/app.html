⬇ Deposit Funds
ℹ About Deposits

Deposits are credited to your account immediately and will appear in your transaction history. There are no fees for deposits. You can deposit to any of your active accounts.

⬆ Withdraw Funds
ℹ About Withdrawals

Withdrawals are processed instantly. Ensure you have sufficient balance before initiating. Withdrawal requests that exceed your balance will be declined automatically.

⇄ Transfer Funds
ℹ About Transfers

Transfers between accounts are instant. You need the target account's ID to proceed. You can find account IDs in the Accounts section. Transfers are irreversible once confirmed.
